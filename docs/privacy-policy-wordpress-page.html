Privacy Policy

Effective date: 21 May 2026

Arewa Scope provides Northern Nigeria news and updates through our website and Android mobile app. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the Arewa Scope mobile app and website.

Information We Collect

The Arewa Scope app mainly displays content from our WordPress website, https://arewascope.com.ng/. We do not require users to create an account inside the app. However, when you use the app or website, some technical information may be processed automatically, such as device type, app version, pages viewed, approximate usage activity, crash information, and basic analytics data.

Push Notifications

The Arewa Scope app may ask for permission to send notifications. We use notifications to send breaking news, latest posts, and important updates. You can deny notification permission or turn off notifications anytime from your phone settings.

Advertising

The app may display advertising, including ads provided by Google AdMob or other advertising partners. Advertising services may use device identifiers, approximate usage signals, diagnostics, and ad interaction data to deliver, limit, personalize, and measure ads where allowed by law. Where required, users may be shown consent options based on their location and applicable law.

Website Content and External Links

The app loads content from Arewa Scope and may open some external links in a browser or another app. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites. Please review the privacy policy of any external website you visit.

Cookies and WebView Data

Because the app displays our WordPress website inside an Android WebView, cookies and similar web technologies may be used for website functionality, analytics, security, and advertising. You may clear app data or browser data from your device settings.

How We Use Information

We use information to provide news content, improve app performance, send notifications when permission is granted, measure basic usage, maintain security, and improve the user experience.

Data Sharing

We do not sell users' personal information. Some technical and usage information may be processed by service providers that help us run the app, website, notifications, analytics, hosting, and advertising.

Children's Privacy

Arewa Scope is a general news service and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Choices

You can turn off push notifications from your Android notification settings. You can also clear app storage or uninstall the app at any time.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when we improve the app, add new features, or comply with new requirements. The updated version will be posted on this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact Arewa Scope through the contact details available on our website: https://arewascope.com.ng/.
